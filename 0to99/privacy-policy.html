Privacy

0 to 99 Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 11, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how information may be handled when you use 0 to 99.

Information collected by the app

0 to 99 does not require account creation and does not ask you to submit personal profile information in order to play.

The app stores gameplay-related settings and progress locally on your device, such as high score, best survival time, input settings, language settings, audio settings, and haptics settings.

Advertising

The app uses Google AdMob to display interstitial advertisements. AdMob and its partners may collect certain device or usage data for ad delivery, fraud prevention, performance measurement, and related advertising operations.

For more information about how Google handles data, please review Google’s policies: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Analytics

The app uses Google Analytics for Firebase to understand how the game is played — for example, when a game starts, scores reached, and which screens are viewed. This may include an app instance identifier along with device and usage data. The information is used in aggregate to measure performance and improve the game.

For more information about how Google handles data, please review Google’s policies: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Local data

Gameplay records and settings are stored locally on your device and are not used to create a user account within the app.

Children’s privacy

The app is intended for a general audience. If platform or regional rules require additional privacy disclosures, those disclosures will be updated here.

Changes to this policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.

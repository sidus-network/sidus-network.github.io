0 to 99 Support

If you need help with 0 to 99, or if you want to report a bug or send feedback, contact us at sidus.network+support@gmail.com.

What to include

  • Your device model and iOS version
  • A short description of the issue
  • Steps to reproduce the problem, if possible

Response

We may not reply immediately to every message, but we review support emails and bug reports as part of ongoing updates.
